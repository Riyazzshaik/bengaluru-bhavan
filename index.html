Our Story

Located in the vibrant heart of Bhimavaram on Town Station Road, Bengaluru Bhavan brings the nostalgic flavors of Bengaluru’s tiffin culture to Andhra Pradesh.

We believe that food is not just about satisfying hunger, but about creating an experience. Our pure vegetarian dishes are crafted using authentic recipes, high-quality ingredients, and a touch of ❤️. From our crispy ghee dosas to the aromatic filter coffee, every bite is a journey to tradition.
